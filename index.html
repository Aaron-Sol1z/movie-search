Movie Search

This application allows you to search for movies and series'. Each search result will display information about the movie or series, including the runtime, genre, plot summary, and other details. This was made to help users find information on a movie or series in a clean and concise section.

Simply enter the title that you want to find information on and hit the 'Get Title' button. You can also include a year to narrow down your search.
